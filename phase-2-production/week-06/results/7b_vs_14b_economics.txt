DECISION FRAMEWORK
==================================================================================================================================

  Question: Is the 14B model worth 2x the GPU cost?

  Latency penalty:     1.38x slower per request
  Throughput penalty:   9.84x fewer total tokens
  Per-GPU efficiency:  3116 tok/s/GPU (7B) vs 158 tok/s/GPU (14B)
  Cost per token:      19.69x more expensive (14B)

  USE 7B WHEN: Latency-sensitive, high-throughput, cost-constrained,
               simple tasks (summarization, extraction, classification)

  USE 14B WHEN: Quality-critical, reasoning-heavy, acceptable latency,
                tasks where wrong answers cost more than GPU time
